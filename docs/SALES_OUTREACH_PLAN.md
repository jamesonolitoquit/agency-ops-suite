# Sales System & Outreach Plan

Purpose: provide a repeatable sales playbook for sourcing leads, qualifying, and converting retained clients.

1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Small to mid-sized businesses needing a simple marketing website + support retainer.
- Industries: food & beverage, local services, professional services.

2. Channels
- Warm outreach: referrals, past clients, networking.
- Cold outreach: targeted email sequences, LinkedIn outreach, and Google Ads landing campaigns.

3. Outreach Sequences
- Sequence example (email): Intro → Case study → Offer to help (value) → CTA for discovery call → Follow-ups (3 total).

4. Qualification
- Use discovery call script: budget, timeline, decision-maker, current provider, must-haves.

5. Proposal & Close
- Use the template registry to attach scope examples. Include clear milestones, acceptance criteria, and payment terms (50% deposit).

6. CRM & Pipeline
- Use a lightweight CRM (Airtable or HubSpot free) with stages: Lead → Contacted → Proposal → Negotiation → Won → Onboarding.

7. Metrics
- Track conversion rate, average deal size, sales cycle length, and MRR from retained clients.

8. Templates
- Create email templates: intro, follow-up, proposal delivery, contract sent, invoice reminder.

9. Outreach Automation
- Use sequence tools (Mailshake, Lemlist) for cold sequences, but keep personal touches for higher-value leads.
