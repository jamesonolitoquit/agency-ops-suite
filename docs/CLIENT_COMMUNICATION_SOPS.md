# Client Communication SOPs

Purpose: standardize client-facing messages, expectations, and handoffs for onboarding and ongoing support.

1. Welcome & Onboarding
- Send a welcome email immediately after contract signing with: SOW, onboarding checklist, kickoff meeting link, and required assets list.
- Kickoff meeting agenda: team intros, objectives, timeline, communication channels, account access, immediate next steps.

2. Meeting Cadence
- Weekly check-ins for active development during launch phases.
- Bi-weekly or monthly review for ongoing maintenance/retainer clients.

3. Response SLAs
- Business hours: responses within 24 hours. Critical incidents: acknowledge within 4 hours and begin triage.

4. Channels
- Primary: Email for formal records and invoices.
- Chat: Slack or Microsoft Teams for day-to-day collaboration (invite at kickoff).
- Support Requests: Client Portal tickets for feature requests and bug reports.

5. Templates
- Welcome email, asset request, weekly status update, release notes, invoice reminder (7/3/0 days), and cancellation confirmation templates.

6. Status Updates
- Weekly status email should include: completed items, in-progress items, blocked items, next steps, and any decisions needed.

7. Escalation & Outages
- Define emergency contact and escalation path in SOW. For outages, update hourly until resolved.

8. Handover Checklist
- List credentials, domains, hosting, backups, documentation, and training materials to deliver at project close or transfer.

9. Documentation & Recordkeeping
- Store all client communication notes in `audit_logs` and ticket history in `client_requests` for traceability.
