# Hosting Agreement (Template)

This Hosting Agreement outlines the terms for hosting services provided by the Agency to the Client.

1. Services
- Provider will host Client websites and related services as described in the SOW.

2. Fees
- Hosting fees are recurring and billed monthly or annually per the SOW.

3. Access & Credentials
- Provider will manage hosting credentials in a secure password manager. Client retains ownership of registrar accounts and may provide agency access as specified.

4. Backups & Recovery
- Provider will perform daily backups and retain them for a minimum of 30 days. Recovery is subject to Provider's support SLA and may incur fees if outside included maintenance.

5. Uptime & SLA
- Target uptime: 99.9%. Scheduled maintenance windows will be communicated 48 hours in advance when possible.

6. Security & Updates
- Provider will apply security updates and patches to hosted environments covered by the agreement. Emergency patches will be applied as needed.

7. Termination & Portability
- On termination, Provider will assist with transferring hosting to Client or a third-party host; transfer fees may apply.

8. Liability
- Provider is not liable for outages caused by third-party providers or client misconfiguration. Provider liability limited to hosting fees paid in the prior 3 months.
