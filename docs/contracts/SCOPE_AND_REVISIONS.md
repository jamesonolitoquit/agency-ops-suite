# Scope, Revisions, and Cancellation Terms

1. Project Scope
- Deliverables and milestones are defined in the Statement of Work (SOW). Any work outside the SOW is considered out-of-scope and will be estimated separately.

2. Revision Limits
- The project includes up to 2 rounds of revisions for deliverables unless otherwise specified. Additional revision rounds are billable.

3. Cancellation
- Client may cancel the project with 14 days written notice. Fees for work completed up to cancellation date are due immediately. Prepaid but unused services will be refunded pro-rata at Provider's discretion.

4. Ownership & Handover
- Ownership of final deliverables transfers to Client upon full payment. Source files and credentials will be provided after final payment and completion of hosting transfer if applicable.

5. Late Payments
- Late payments incur interest of 1.5% per month and may result in suspension of services after 15 days past due.
